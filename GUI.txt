Simpole pipeline GUI
Frame with two switching menus for encode and decode.
Encode menu:
    input radiobutton: infile or process(any)
    input file/process: txt field with filename to encode or process command line
    qrve options: txt field
    v-compressor command: executable name specified(if none generate to output file)
    v-compressor options: txt field
    outfile: txt field with fn(if none generate stdout)
    
Decode menu:
    input radiobutton: infile or process(v-decompressor)
    
    input file: txt field with filename to decode
    or
    v-decompressor command: executable name specified
    v-decompressor options: txt field
    
    qrvd options: txt field
    outfile: txt field with fn(if none generate stdout)

Main button: Start/Stop

CFG file with common options for qrve/d and vcompressor/decompressor options
